A biennial herb (Daucus carota of the family Umbelliferae, the carrot family) with a usually orange spindle-shaped edible root.

Healthy

A root vegetable often claimed to be the perfect health food. It is crunchy, tasty, and hightly nutritious. Carrots are a particularly good source of beta carotene, fiber, vitamin K1, potassium, and antioxidants.

Carat?

Carrot is sometimes confused with carat, a unit of weight for precious stones and pearls, now equivalent to 200 milligrams.
